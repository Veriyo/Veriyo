Veriyo Partner Programme Agreement

Last updated: July 2026

By ticking "I agree" when applying to the Veriyo Partner Programme, you confirm you have read and accept the terms below.

1. How Commissions Work

You earn a percentage of the monthly subscription fee paid by any workshop that signs up using your referral link or code — but only for that workshop's first 12 months as a paying subscriber. From month 13 onward, that workshop's payments belong entirely to Veriyo and no longer generate a commission for you.

The exact percentage, minimum payout threshold, and a full worked example are shown in your Partner Dashboard under "How Earnings Will Work" once you're signed in. That page is kept up to date and takes precedence over any example figures quoted elsewhere.

2. Veriyo's Founding Period

As of your sign-up date, all workshops on Veriyo are on a free "founding" plan. Any "estimated earnings" figure shown in your dashboard during this period is illustrative only and is not a promise of payment. Nothing is owed to you, and no payment will be made, until Veriyo introduces paid workshop plans and a workshop you referred actually pays for one.

3. If You Leave the Programme

If you leave the programme, or your account is deactivated, you forfeit any right to future commissions on your referrals from that point forward. Commissions already earned from a workshop's actual paid subscription, prior to your departure, will still be honoured. Estimated or projected earnings that had not yet converted to a real, paid subscription are not owed to you.

4. Independent Partner Status

This is not an employment relationship. You are an independent partner, not a Veriyo employee, and are responsible for your own tax obligations on any commission earned.

5. Changes to This Agreement

Veriyo may update this agreement as the programme develops. You'll be notified of material changes. Continuing to participate in the programme after a change takes effect constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

Questions about this agreement can be sent to privacy@veriyo.co.za.

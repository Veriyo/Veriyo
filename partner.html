Partner Handbook

Veriyo Mission

Veriyo helps South African motorists make informed decisions about vehicle repairs by providing transparent pricing data and verified workshop information. We believe every motorist deserves to know what fair pricing looks like.

Partner Responsibilities

  • Represent Veriyo honestly and accurately
  • Share your referral link in relevant communities
  • Answer questions from motorists professionally
  • Complete daily tasks and submit activity reports
  • Report any issues to the admin team

Daily Workflow

  1. Login - Check your Tasks page for today's objectives
  2. Share - Post your referral link in relevant groups
  3. Engage - Have conversations with interested motorists
  4. Complete Tasks - Mark each task as done
  5. Submit Report - Fill in your daily activity report

What NOT to Do

  • Do not post in unrelated or irrelevant groups
  • Do not make promises about specific earnings
  • Do not misrepresent Veriyo's services or guarantees
  • Do not share personal information of other partners
  • Do not use fake accounts or deceptive practices

How Referrals Work

When someone clicks your referral link:

  1. A cookie tracks their visit to your account
  2. If they submit a motorist report, you're credited
  3. If they register a workshop, you're credited
  4. When a workshop is approved, you earn

Communication Standards

  • Be polite and professional in all interactions
  • Respond to messages within 24 hours when possible
  • If you don't know an answer, say so and find out
  • Never argue with motorists - direct them to support

Support

If you need help, use the Support tab to submit a request. The admin team will respond as soon as possible. For urgent matters, use the Chat feature to speak directly with an administrator.

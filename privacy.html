Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who We Are

Veriyo is a South African consumer transparency platform that allows vehicle owners to share and access information about car repair pricing at workshops across South Africa. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA).

For all privacy-related enquiries, contact us at: privacy.veriyo@proton.me

2. What Information We Collect

We collect different information depending on how you use Veriyo.

When you submit a repair report:

  • Workshop name, suburb, and city
  • Vehicle make, model, and year
  • Date of repair
  • Type of repair and part or service description
  • Amount quoted and amount paid
  • Your rating and additional notes
  • An invoice image, if you choose to upload one

When you list a workshop:

  • Workshop name and physical address
  • Contact number and email address
  • Operating hours and specialisations
  • Service pricing information
  • Years in operation and accreditation status

3. Why We Collect It

We collect this information for one purpose: to build a transparent, community-driven record of vehicle repair pricing in South Africa. This helps South African vehicle owners make informed decisions before visiting a workshop and protects them from unexpected or inflated costs.

We do not use your information for marketing. We do not sell your information to any third party. We do not use your information for any purpose beyond what is described in this policy.

4. Who We Share Your Information With

We use two third-party services to operate this platform. Both are based outside South Africa. By using Veriyo, you acknowledge that your information may be processed in these jurisdictions.

Supabase Inc (United States)

All submission data is stored on Supabase's database servers. Supabase is our data storage provider. Your submission details are saved to and retrieved from Supabase's infrastructure. Supabase operates under applicable United States data protection standards.

ntfy.sh (Germany)

When a new submission is received, a notification is sent through ntfy.sh to alert the platform administrator. This notification contains the workshop name, city, repair type, and amount paid from your submission. It does not contain your personal identity.

5. Published Information

Submissions that are approved by our moderation team are displayed publicly on the Browse Prices page. Published submissions include the workshop name, suburb, city, vehicle details, repair type, amount paid, rating, and notes. We do not publish any information that identifies you personally as the person who submitted the report.

All published submissions represent the personal experiences and opinions of the individuals who submitted them. Veriyo does not independently verify the factual accuracy of individual submissions. Published content should be read as community experience reports, not as independently verified facts.

6. Invoice Images

If you upload an invoice image with your submission, it is stored securely and used only to support the review of your submission. Invoice images are not published publicly. If your submission is approved, only the information you entered into the form fields is displayed — not the image itself.

7. How Long We Keep Your Information

Repair submissions are kept for as long as they remain relevant to the platform's purpose of providing pricing transparency to South African consumers. Workshop listings are kept until the business requests removal or until the listing is no longer accurate. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us at the address in section 1.

8. Your Rights Under POPIA

The Protection of Personal Information Act gives you the following rights regarding your personal information held by Veriyo:

  • Right to access — you may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to correction — you may request that inaccurate information be corrected
  • Right to deletion — you may request that your personal information be deleted from our records
  • Right to object — you may object to the processing of your personal information

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy.veriyo@proton.me. We will respond within a reasonable time and in any event within 30 days.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect the personal information we hold. Data is stored on Supabase's secure infrastructure with access restricted to authorised administrators only. If you become aware of any security concern related to your information on this platform, contact us immediately at privacy.veriyo@proton.me.

10. Complaints

If you believe we have handled your personal information in a way that violates POPIA, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa.

Information Regulator (South Africa)
Website: www.inforeg.org.za
Email: inforeg@justice.gov.za

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform grows or as legal requirements change. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last updated. Continued use of Veriyo after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

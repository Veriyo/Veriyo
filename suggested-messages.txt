Partner Guidelines

Partners should avoid:

❌ "Best workshop website"

❌ "Limited offer"

❌ "Register now"

❌ "Only today"

❌ "You are losing money"

❌ "Guaranteed savings"

❌ "Cheapest"

❌ Copying and pasting the same message into dozens of groups.

Instead they should:

Adjust the wording slightly for each group.
Mention Veriyo only once in each message.
Focus on solving one problem.
Keep messages under 120 words where possible.
Only post in relevant community, neighbourhood, motoring, or local business groups.
Answer questions honestly instead of trying to "sell" Veriyo. Building trust first leads to stronger engagement over time.
